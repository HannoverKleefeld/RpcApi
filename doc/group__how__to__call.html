Call a function.

There are two ways to make calls

  1. the first possibilities which is also standard
    $api = new RpcApi('demo_config.json', new RpcLogger());
    $volume=$api->GetVolume();
  2. the second possibilities
    $api = new RpcApi('demo_config.json', new RpcLogger());
    $volume = $api->__call('GetVolume', []);
